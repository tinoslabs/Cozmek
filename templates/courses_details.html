Course Descriptions

This comprehensive Data Science and AI Masterclass will take you from beginner to advanced level in the most in-demand skills of the 21st century. You'll learn Python programming, statistical analysis, machine learning, deep learning, and AI implementation through hands-on projects with real-world datasets.

Requirements for The Course

This course is designed for beginners, but some basic math knowledge (high school level) will be helpful. No prior programming experience is required, though familiarity with basic computer operations is expected

  • A computer with internet access (Windows, Mac or Linux)
  • Basic high school level mathematics
  • Willingness to learn and solve problems
  • No prior programming experience needed
