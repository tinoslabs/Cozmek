Online and Offline Sessions?

Yes, Cozmek primarily focuses on online training, ensuring flexibility and accessibility for learners across the globe. However, we also offer offline classroom sessions at select locations for students who prefer in-person learning.

Does Cozmek provide placement assistance or job guarantees?

Yes, Cozmek offers placement assistance to help students prepare for job opportunities after completing their courses. This includes resume building, interview preparation, mock interviews, and access to job openings through our network.

Does Cozmek offer training in a wide range of technologies?

Absolutely! While Cozmek specializes in Data Science and Python Programming, we are continually expanding our offerings to include a wide range of in-demand technologies.
